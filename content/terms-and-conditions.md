**NANPANTAN SCOUT GROUP BUILDING**

**Booking Form and Hire Agreement including Conditions of Hire for the
premises of Nanpantan Scout Group**

This agreement is made between:

a)  Nanpantan Scout Group ("**NSG**")

and

b)  the "**Hirer**"

NSG agrees for the permit the Hirer to use the premises or parts of
premise(s) designated below for the purposes and period(s) at the agreed
fee, subject to the conditions of hire outlined below.

**STANDARD CONDITIONS OF HIRE**

For the purposes of these conditions, the term **HIRER** shall mean an
individual hirer or, where the hirer is an organisation, the authorised
representative. The term **NSG** means the Nanpantan Scout Group.

The Nanpantan Scout Group building may be hired up until 10pm each day
(11pm on a Saturday). Hire ends at these designated end times and
adequate time to clear away should therefore be allowed at the end of
your event. The period of hire must include adequate time for setting up
and clearing away.

Payment for one off bookings is required with the deposit at the time of
booking. Recurring bookings can be secured by a one off payment of a
month's hire. Thereafter, invoices will be sent monthly, in advance, and
payment due within 28 days. For the rates of hire please refer to our
hire rates schedule.

Hire rates are subject to review every 12 months.

Hire of the hall is subject to the following terms and conditions:

1.  **Supervision**

The hirer will, during the period of the hiring, be responsible for
supervision of the premises, the fabric and the contents: their care,
safety from damage however slight: or change of any sort and the
behaviour of all persons using the premises whatever their capacity:
including proper supervision of car parking arrangements (no parking on
patio on grassed areas) and avoid obstructing traffic on Nanpantan Road
with double parking. The hirer should also ensure that the outer doors
of the hall are kept closed at all times for security reasons. There is
no access to the upstairs of the building unless the meeting room has
been booked.

2.  **Use of the Premises**

The hirer shall not use the premises for any purpose other than that
described in the hiring agreement and shall not sub-hire or use the
premises or allow the premises to be used for any unlawful purpose or in
any unlawful way nor do anything or bring onto the premises anything
which may endanger the same or render invalid any insurance policies in
respect thereof.

3.  **Posters/ Ballons/ Bunting**

Bunting or ballons can be attached with string to the hooks on the main
hall. No use of Blue tac, Sellotape, drawings pins (or similar) to
attach items to the fabric of the building.

4.  **Occupancy**

The hirer is responsible for ensuring there is no breech of the
occupancy levels for the building (maximum number 242 people in the main
hall, 41 in the upstairs meeting room).

5.  **Noise**

The hirer shall take responsibility for monitoring of noise inside and
outside the building during their period of hire (including when people
are leaving the building and car park) and take remedial action to
mitigate impact to neighbours. The hirer should, at all times, comply
with all aspects of the Noise Management Plan for the building.

6.  **Music**

The hirer should ensure that that windows and doors are shut if
amplified music or sound is played inside the building. No amplified
music outside the building at any time, low level music permissible
outside between 8am and 6pm.

7.  **Alcohol**

No sale of alcohol without a Temporary Event Licence from Charnwood
Borough Council. Alcohol consumption by persons under the age of 18 must
not be permitted.

8.  **Gaming, Betting and Lotteries**

The hirer shall ensure that nothing is done on or in relation to the
premises in contravention of the law relating to gaming, betting and
lotteries.

9.  **Public Safety Compliance**

The hirer shall comply with all conditions and regulations made in
respect of the premises by the Fire Authority, Local Authority, the
Local Magistrates' Court or otherwise, particularly in connection with
any event which includes public dancing or music or other similar public
entertainment or stage plays.

Compliance with existing Gov.UK regulations relevant to the health and
safety of users is expected. Event risk assessments should be undertaken
where required and the hirer is responsible for ensuring compliance with
specific criteria, including the number of attendees and any relevant
criteria during the hire period.

10. **Health and Hygiene**

The hirer shall, if preparing, serving or selling food observe all
relevant food health and hygiene legislation and regulations.

11. **Smoking**

The hirer shall ensure that no smoking or vaping takes place anywhere in
the building. This includes the use of e-cigarettes.

12. **Electrical Appliance Safety**

The hirer shall ensure that any electrical appliances brought on to the
premises must be tested under the usual regulations and be safe and in
good working order.

13. **Indemnity**

The hirer shall indemnify NSG for the cost of repair of any damage done
to any part of the property including the curtilage thereof or the
contents of the buildings which may occur during the period of the
hiring as a result of the hiring.

14. **Insurance**

The hirer shall be responsible for making arrangements to insure against
any third party claims which may lie against him or her (or the
organisation if acting as a representative) whilst using the building.
(NSG is insured against any claims arising out of its own negligence).

15. **Accidents and Dangerous Occurrences**

The hirer must report all accidents involving injury to any party during
the period using the accident form in the kitchen. The Group Lead
Volunteer should also be notified as soon as is practically possible
either via email (carltravis-roberts@nanpantanscouts.org.uk) or phone
using the emergency contact details displayed in the building.

Any failure of equipment either that belonging to the hall or brought in
by the hirer must also be reported as soon as possible to the Group Lead
Volunteer, details as above.

16. **Animals**

The hirer shall ensure that no animals (including birds) except guide
dogs are brought into the hall, other than for a special event agreed to
by NSG. And no animals (other than Assistance dogs) whatsoever are to
enter the kitchen at any time.

17. **Children**

The hirer shall ensure that children are supervised at all times.
Strictly no unsupervised children in the kitchen.

18. **Bouncy Castles/ Inflatables**

Bouncy Castles or similar inflatables allowed inside and outside the
building subject to the following conditions

-   Any inflatables outside to be positioned to minimise intrusion to
    neighbouring properties and to be deflated by 9pm

-   Hirer to ensure supervision at all times and suitable insurance from
    the supplier of the inflatable

-   Maximum permissible inflatable size 5m x 5m (height 4m)

Generators to be sited away from boundaries with neighbours.

19. **Fireworks**

No fireworks permitted at any time

20. **Permissible Items**

No use of smoke, foam or bubble making machines. Glitter/ confetti can
be used inside the building but the Hirer must ensure this is fully
cleared up at the end of the hire.

21. **BBQs**

No use of disposable barbeques. Other barbeques to only be set up on the
patio.

22. **Fire**

Hirer to make themselves familiar with fire exits and location of fire
extinguishers. The building does have a fire alarm, in the event this
was to sound, the Hirer to ensure everyone proceeds to the muster point
(by the telegraph pole in the car park) and alert the emergency
authorities in the event of a fire and then the NSG emergency contact.

23. **Publicity**

The hirer shall not carry out or permit fly posting or any other form of
unauthorised advertisements for any event taking place at the Hall, and
shall indemnify the NSG accordingly against all action, claims and
proceedings arising from any breach of this condition. Failure to
observe this condition may lead to prosecution by the local authority.

24. **Sale of Goods**

The hirer shall, if selling goods on the premises, comply with Fair
Trading Laws and any code of practice used in connection with such
sales. In particular, the hirer shall ensure that the total prices of
all goods and services are prominently displayed, as shall be the
organiser's name and address and that any discounts offered are based
only on Manufacturer's Recommended Retail Prices.

25. **WiFi policy**

Reference should be made to our WiFi policy which is published on our
website and also displayed on the notice board at the Hall.

26. **Cancellation**

Cancellation must be notified 4 weeks in advance of a booking for a full
refund of the booking fee.

27. **Unfit for Use**

In the event of the hall or any part thereof being rendered unfit for
the use for which it has been hired, the Committee shall not be liable
to the hirer for any resulting loss or damage whatsoever.

28. **Refusal of Booking**

NSG reserves the right to refuse a booking without notice or to cancel
this hiring agreement at any time whether before or during the term of
the agreement upon giving 7 days notice in writing to the hirer. The
hirer shall be entitled upon such notice to reimbursement of such monies
including the deposit or a proportion of the same as have been paid by
the hirer to the NSG. NSG shall not be liable to make any further
payment to the hirer.

29. **End of Hire**

The hirer shall be responsible for leaving the premises and surrounding
area in a clean and tidy condition, shutting doors and windows,
switching off lights and appliances, properly locking the building (key
returned to key safe) and secured unless directed otherwise. Any
contents temporarily removed from their usual positions properly
replaced, otherwise the NSG shall be at liberty to make an additional
charge.

It is the responsibility of the hirer to take all rubbish away with
them, recycling can be put in the external recycling bin (or take home
if full). Your deposit will be returned within 5 working days once NSG
are satisfied that all the above terms and conditions have been duly
complied with by the hirer. When stacking and putting away the chairs
and tables or moving any equipment around the hall before, during or
after hire please refrain from scraping anything against the interior
walls. A fee for any damage to the paintwork will be deducted from your
deposit.

No responsibility whatsoever can be taken for any personal belongings
used or left in the NSG building.
